\frametitle{Hajek Weights for ATT Estimation}

The Hajek-weighted ATT estimator under conditional parallel trends:

\[ \hat{\tau}^{\text{Hajek}} = \frac{1}{N_{\text{treated}}}
   \sum_{i: D_i = 1}
   \left[ Y_i(1) - \frac{\hat{p}(X_i)}{1-\hat{p}(X_i)} Y_i(0) \right] \]

where $\hat{p}(X_i)$ is the propensity score and $D_i$ is the treatment indicator.
